Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our automatic gate solutions and services.

Our professional installation team typically completes most residential gate installations within 1-2 days, depending on the complexity of your property and existing infrastructure. We begin with a comprehensive site assessment to understand your specific needs and any potential challenges. For commercial properties or complex configurations, we may require additional time. We always provide a detailed timeline estimate during your initial consultation. Our team works efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily activities. We handle all necessary permits and inspections as part of our service.

Our automatic gate systems are engineered for minimal maintenance. We recommend basic care such as keeping the gate clean, checking moving parts for debris, and lubricating hinges and rollers twice yearly. Most electronic components are sealed and weather-resistant, requiring no special attention. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages ranging from annual inspections to quarterly service visits, depending on your needs. These preventive maintenance visits help identify potential issues before they become problems, extending the lifespan of your system. Battery backup systems should be tested periodically to ensure they function properly during power outages. Our technicians can handle all maintenance tasks, or we can train your team to perform basic checks.

Absolutely! Our modern automatic gate systems are designed to work seamlessly with popular smart home platforms including Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Samsung SmartThings. This integration allows you to control your gate alongside other smart devices in your home. You can create automation routines, such as opening the gate when you arrive home or closing it at sunset. The system also supports IFTTT (If This Then That) for advanced automation scenarios. For security cameras and alarm systems, we offer direct integration options that enable coordinated responses—for example, your gate can automatically lock when your alarm system is armed. Our technical team can guide you through the integration process and ensure everything works perfectly with your existing setup.

Our automatic gate systems include built-in battery backup systems that ensure your gate continues to operate during power outages. The backup battery provides enough power for multiple gate cycles, allowing you and authorized users to enter and exit your property safely. The system automatically switches to battery power when it detects a power loss, and most modern systems can operate for 24-48 hours depending on usage. Additionally, all our gates feature manual override options, so even if the battery is completely depleted, you can manually operate the gate if necessary. We recommend having the battery system tested during your annual maintenance visit to ensure it's functioning properly. For extended outages or critical security needs, we offer optional generator integration that can provide indefinite power supply to your gate system.

Yes, all Dubya automatic gate systems are fully compliant with Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) standards. We design our gates with universal accessibility in mind, featuring multiple access methods to accommodate people with varying abilities. Our systems include push-button controls positioned at accessible heights, voice command compatibility for hands-free operation, smartphone app control for remote access, and proximity sensors for automatic opening. The gates operate at safe speeds and include obstacle detection to prevent accidents. We also ensure adequate clear space for wheelchairs and other mobility devices. Our design meets all federal accessibility guidelines and local building codes. During the consultation phase, we assess your specific accessibility needs and customize the system accordingly. We're committed to ensuring that everyone, regardless of physical ability, can access your property safely and independently.
